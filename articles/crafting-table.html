Lego Crafting Table (21265) Celebrating 15 years of Minecraft

7th June 2024

15 years after Minecraft was released, Lego has finally released an 18+ Minecraft-themed set that allows you to own and display your very own Minecraft world.

This 1195-piece set will be retailing for £79.99/US$89.99/€89.99/CA$119.99 and is available to pre-order ready for the official release on April 1 on Lego.com.

  • 21265 Crafting Table (UK) ~ £79.99
  • 21265 Crafting Table (US) ~ US$89.99
  • 21265 Crafting Table (AUS) ~ AUS$129.99
  • 21265 Crafting Table (CA) ~ CA$119.99

For an 18+ set, I’m happy to see it on the more reasonable end of the price range for Lego as adult sets, especially licensed ones, can typically be hundreds of pounds.

Even though this set is primarily aimed at adults, it is going to be popular and appreciated by Minecraft fans of all ages.

The Crafting Table contains 5 mini builds, which in total come together to form 12 biomes. There are 8 micro figures included which can inhabit your world, or be displayed on small podiums which can be personalised with your favourite quotes from the game! (Fortunately these are the only stickers included).

I love that this set looks great from any angle, and can even have the top removed for an even better view of the interior.

Overall The Crafting Table looks to be both an excellent and affordable set Minecraft fans can get excited for August 1st.
