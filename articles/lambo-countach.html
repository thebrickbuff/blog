Lamborghini Countach 5000 Quattrovalvole (10337) Latest Edition to Lego Icons

Published 7th June 2024

The latest edition to the Lego cars lineup is the instantly recognisable classic, the Lamborghini Countach 5000 Quattrovalvole. The Countach line originally began in the 70s and was considered one of the first supercars of the time. The Lamborghini Countach 5000 Quattrovalvole was released slightly later during the 80's.

This 1506-piece set will be retailing for £159.99/US$179.99/€299.99/CA$239.99 and is available to Lego Insiders from the 1st till the 3rd of July before being officially released on the 4th of July on Lego.com.

  • 10337 Lamborghini Countach 5000 Quattrovalvole (UK) ~ £159.99
  • 10337 Lamborghini Countach 5000 Quattrovalvole (US) ~ US$179.99
  • 10337 Lamborghini Countach 5000 Quattrovalvole (AUS) ~ AUS$299.99
  • 10337 Lamborghini Countach 5000 Quattrovalvole (CA) ~ CA$239.99

The car has an iconic geometric shape, which the Lego designers have done an incredible job replicating in the model, whilst keeping it to scale with the other Technic/Icons cars. This allows it to be seamlessly integrated into your display.

As well as nailing the exterior, the car boasts a detailed interior, packed with detail ranging from the cockpit to the V12 engine.

Lego managed to include some functionality into the model with an opening hood/trunk, and working steering along with the must-have vertically opening doors.

This is a must-have vehicle for any car enthusiast!

Interestingly, the Speed Champions 76908 Lamborghini Countach set is currently available. If you have your heart set on this model but require a more budget and/or space-friendly option, this could be the set for you. Just bear in mind that the smaller model is lacking in much of the detail and functionality.
