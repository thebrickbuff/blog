A Fresh Take On Lego Star Wars: Creative Play Droid Builder Revealed

  • 75392 Creative Play Droid Builder (US) ~ US$99.99
  • 75392 Creative Play Droid Builder (AUS) ~ AUS$169.99
  • 75392 Creative Play Droid Builder (CA) ~ CA$129.99

The latest set to join the Lego Star Wars August 1st lineup is the 75392 Creative Play Droid Builder, which has finally been revealed. The Creative Play Droid Builder set contains the final of the 25th-anniversary figures, young Leia, alongside her trusty companion L0-LA59, aka Lola.

The set contains 4 brick-built astromechs, R2-D2, Chopper, QT-KT and R5-J2 along with a multitude of exciting accessories allowing you to customize your droids however you please.

A fun new concept for Lego Star Wars, it's definitely a set aimed at a younger audience which I don't think will be overly popular with the adult fan base. Personally, I feel the droids look a little too blocky and wouldn't quite look right on display, especially as you can get an incredible buildable R2-D2 (75379) with the 25th-anniversary figure of Darth Malek for the same price.

The Young Leia figure would also not have been my first choice for the last of the anniversary figures, however, I'm glad that Lola is also included, to give the figure a little more detail. With that being said I love how detailed Lola is, but I feel that she is massively oversized and could have been printed onto a 1x1 circular tile like the thermal detonators instead for a more in-scale representation.

Even though this is a set I will probably not be looking to pick up myself (I may pick up Young Leia to complete the collection), it's definitely a great buy for younger fans!
